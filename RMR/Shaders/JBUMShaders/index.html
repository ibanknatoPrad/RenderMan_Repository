JBUMcraters.sl

Lunar-like surface shader (with displacements). The shader uses cellnoise along with fBm to make craters. This shader makes use of the following Advanced RenderMan include files: material.h, noises.h, and patterns.h.

Also, the RIB example make use of LGStarfield.sl
