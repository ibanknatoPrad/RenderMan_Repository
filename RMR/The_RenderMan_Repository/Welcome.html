The RenderMan® Repository (RMR)

 

A central resource for subjects related to RenderMan®.

Welcome to the first primary web site that is dedicated entirely in sharing information on RenderMan.  Work on it started in 1994 and was online in early 1995.


What is RenderMan®? 

Find out on the Wiki pages.


The site has always been open to others to contribute.  In the hope of making this easier, The site now has a Wiki.  If you want access to add content to the Wiki, either you need to be somebody, I know; be sponsored by somebody that I know; or have two RenderMan shaders submitted and approved.
