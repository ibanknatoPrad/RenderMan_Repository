User Contributions

Call for contributions:

Have you:

  • Improved or extended the shaders or library routines presented in Advanced RenderMan?
  • Used the book materials to make particularly spectacular images?
  • Written shaders or library routines of similar generality and clear explanation?

If so, submit them to lg@bmrt.org and if deemed worthy I'll put them on this page so that other users can benefit from your infinite wisdom.

Word of warning: I will be very picky about what I actually put up on this page. I'm really interested in contributions whose quality, generality, and documentation are comparable to the code in Chapters 9-12. I will also show preference to contributions that stick to the style and conventions in the book (for example, if you contribute an illumination model, I would prefer that it stick to the conventions fo the LocIllum and Material functions of Chapter 9).
